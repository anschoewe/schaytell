Welcome

Hello! As you might have guessed, we are betrothed! Hopefully you know us already (otherwise, it’s a little creepy that you’re here….), but this site will provide you with all you need to know about our upcoming nuptials. We’re excited to get married this October in NY’s Hudson Valley, and are thrilled that each of you will be joining in our celebration. We hope the weekend will be full of food, fun and fall foliage.

Last June, we got engaged in the Hudson Valley, over s’mores at Devon’s parent’s house (hence those gooey Save the Dates). With that, the office romance that began at the intersection of strategy and organization blossomed into a lifetime of love.

We’ll update this as we get closer, etc, etc. please feel free to reach out to us with any questions, concerns or XXXX: EMAILS.
